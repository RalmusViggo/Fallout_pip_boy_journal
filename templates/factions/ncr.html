New California Republic

The New California Republic (NCR) is a post-War federal republic founded in the namesake territory of New California in 2189. At its peak, its territory comprised five contiguous states located in southern California,[8][5] with additional holdings in northern California,[16][17] Oregon,[RPG 1] Nevada,[18][19][RPG 2] Arizona, and northern Mexico.
