The Effect of radiation (Glow) on Ghouls (and all other thing) .

Radiation's effect on ghouls

Glow Accumulation

When a ghoul’s physical condition is fully restored, additional radiation exposure produces a stored bio-energetic effect commonly referred to as Glow.

Glow functions as a secondary reserve of vitality layered on top of the subject’s normal health capacity. In addition to providing additional durability, this stored radiation may serve as a resource for certain physiological reactions and abilities observed in some ghouls.

The maximum quantity of Glow that can be retained is roughly equivalent to the individual’s full health capacity.

Glow accumulation occurs through exposure to radioactive environments, the consumption of irradiated substances, or contact with radiation-emitting technologies. The exact amount gained varies depending on the source and intensity of the radiation.

Because Glow relies on stored radiation, substances or technologies designed to cleanse radiation from the body will remove it. Individuals relying on Glow therefore typically avoid radiation-purging treatments and minimize radiation resistance that would reduce absorption.

Radiation Metabolism

A defining biological characteristic of ghouls is their extreme tolerance to ionizing radiation. Instead of causing cellular damage, radiation is partially metabolized as a restorative stimulus.

Approximately 15% of incoming radiation exposure is converted into regenerative healing. This allows ghouls to survive in environments that would rapidly kill most other organisms.

Direct exposure to catastrophic radiation events remains lethal during the moment of detonation due to the immense physical forces involved.

Mutational Compatibility

Ghoul physiology remains compatible with many forms of mutation. However, spontaneous mutation through radiation exposure does not occur in the same way it does in non-ghoul organisms. Mutations must instead be introduced through specialized biochemical treatments.

Mutations may also be removed through conventional radiation-cleansing procedures.

Not all mutations provide equal benefits to ghoul physiology. Some offer little advantage or may even become functionally irrelevant due to the unique biological traits of ghouls.

Other Things

Feral Degradation

Upon undergoing full ghoulification, the biological requirements of hunger and thirst cease to function in a conventional manner. In their place emerges a psychological and neurological stability measure commonly referred to as the feral state threshold. This measure reflects the gradual erosion of higher cognitive function associated with advanced ghoul degeneration.

The threshold naturally declines over time but may be partially restored through the consumption of various chemical compounds. Most chemical stimulants or stabilizers provide some degree of restoration, though potency varies between substances. Certain specialized stabilizing compounds exist that are particularly effective at restoring lost stability.

The degradation rate is gradual under normal conditions, declining at an approximate rate equivalent to 1% every ten minutes. As stability decreases, physiological and behavioral changes begin to manifest. Some individuals report increased physical aggression and resilience at lower stability levels, though these effects come at the cost of cognitive control and social function.

The general stages of feral degradation are summarized below:

  • Above 75% Stability

    Noticeable increases in physical strength and endurance, accompanied by improved overall vitality.

  • Between 75% and 50% Stability

    Moderate improvements to physical resilience remain present.

  • Between 50% and 25% Stability

    Early neurological degradation begins to manifest, reducing endurance and overall vitality.

  • Below 25% Stability

    Severe physiological deterioration occurs, significantly reducing endurance, stamina, and resilience.

  • At 0% Stability

    The subject enters a near-feral behavioral state. Physical aggression increases dramatically, while higher reasoning, social capability, coordination, and fine motor accuracy are heavily impaired.

Even at complete stability loss, the condition is not necessarily permanent. Restoration of stability remains possible through sufficient chemical intervention.

Disease Immunity

Ghouls display a near-complete immunity to conventional diseases. Their heavily mutated cellular structure appears hostile to most pathogens, preventing infection from taking hold.

As a result, ghouls neither contract nor carry common diseases. This immunity eliminates both the risks and potential biological interactions associated with disease processes.

Social Interaction Effects

Despite retaining intelligence and personality in non-feral states, ghouls frequently encounter social barriers when interacting with other populations. Their altered appearance and the widespread fear of feral degeneration often lead to mistrust or hostility.

This social stigma can significantly impair negotiation, persuasion, and cooperative interactions. In many regions, ghouls face restricted access to certain communities, organizations, or secured facilities.

To mitigate these difficulties, some individuals rely on concealment methods such as disguises or protective clothing designed to obscure visible signs of ghoulification. When effectively concealed, ghouls are often able to interact with others without immediately triggering suspicion.
