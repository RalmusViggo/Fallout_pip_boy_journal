About me and my journal.

My name is Mark Schultz. I am often called "The Chronicler".

I started writing this journal in the year 2163. I began writing it because I was so very fascinated by how humans and other creatures have adapted to the radioactive wasteland that is the world.

As you might have been able to pice together if you've read some of my entries, I am far above the age of really any human, and this is because I am a ghoul.

The current year as of when I'm writing this is 2287. So I am 148 years as of when I'm writing this.

This journal is a guide of sorts I've made, I am... or was (depending on if I'm still in ownership of this pip-boy) hoping to make this a replicable holotape, so it can be widely distributed.


If you have found this on my corpse, please be careful with it, it is very precious to me. If it wasn't on a corpse, still be careful with it, but there should be a tracking signal that should be broadcasting on an unused radio frequency, the frequency is 85.3. Please use it to find me and return my journal to me.


Yours truly, Schultz
